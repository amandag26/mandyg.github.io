About Me

 Hello my name is Amanda Garibay. I reside in Atlanta, GA and have lived here for almost 6 years. You may catch me walking my dog and eating tacos in Midtown. I enjoy living in the city and being surrounded by so many companies that are tech heavy and also encourage women to pursue tech careers. I am currently finishing my BS in Graphic and Multimedia Design at DeVry University. Yay!


 I have had a passion for art since I was little and decided to pursue it as a career. I have a strong interest in graphic design, web design, 3D modeling and animation. I have a strong passion to learn new skills and strengthen my current skills. I consider myself a novice in Illustrator, Photoshop, Maya, and Premiere Pro. I am pursuing certification as well as my degree to strengthen my knowledge of Illustrator, Photoshop, and Maya. I hope to work for an animation company in the future and see my name in the credits of a movie or television show.
